Angular material custom color demo
  1. Create a new Angular project (ng new XXXX)
  2. When prompted, choose .scss for style sheets
  3. Add material library (ng add @angular/material)
  4. When prompted, choose 'custom scheme' instead of one of the four predefined schemes
  5. Define your own color palettes (primary & accent should be sufficient) @ http://mcg.mbitson.com
  6. Create a stylesheet with your custom color palettes and import into styles.scss
  7. Create your custom theme in styles.scss
  8. To use dark theme, replace mat-light-theme() with mat-dark-theme() and add mat-app-background to <body> of your index.html
    

If you are not a designer, be careful with colors or you may mess up!
